# StudySprint AI

## Project Overview

StudySprint AI is a smart study planning platform that helps students create personalized study schedules based on their goals, exam dates, subjects, and available study hours.

The platform aims to improve consistency, reduce procrastination, and help students prepare efficiently for exams.

---

## Problem Statement

Many students struggle to create realistic study plans and maintain consistency throughout their preparation journey. Existing solutions often provide generic schedules that do not adapt to individual needs.

---

## Proposed Solution

StudySprint AI generates personalized study plans based on user inputs such as exam date, subjects, and available study time.

The platform also tracks progress and helps students stay on schedule.

---

## Target Users

* School Students
* College Students
* Competitive Exam Aspirants
* Self-Learners

---

## Core Features

### Study Goal Setup

Students enter:

* Exam Name
* Exam Date
* Subjects
* Daily Study Hours

### Personalized Study Plan

The system generates a customized study schedule.

### Daily Task Breakdown

Tasks are divided into manageable daily goals.

### Progress Tracking

Students can mark tasks as completed and track progress.

### Smart Rescheduling

Missed tasks can be redistributed across future study sessions.
